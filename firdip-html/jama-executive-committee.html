The Executive Committee

The Executive Committee consists of eight (8) members who are elected and empowered by the General Body Committee to oversee the daily activities of the committee and ensure enforcement and compliance of the policies and procedures. Its members are: JAMA’A Chairman, JAMA’A Vice Chairman, the Treasurer, and the Five Main-Committees Chairmen in addition to JAMA’A Coordinator. Membership term is three years and can be extended for similar term or longer with the
consent of both parties and the concurrence of the member’s employer. The mission of the Executive Committee is to oversee that the daily activities and responsibilities are carried out and to ensure that JAMA’A Procedures and guidelines are implemented as set forth by the members. Also part of its responsibilities is to develop goals and work plan for JAMA’A and insure their implementation
